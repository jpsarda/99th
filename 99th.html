Work in progress... One button racing / score game. Become 1st as fast as possible. Touching your opponents slows you down. Official place to play the game with highscores : 99th on BonsuLevel.org.

99th is an html5 game by @jpsarda made with cocos2d-html5. Score handled with ScoreOid. Twitter login handled with tmhOAuth. It's my january entry for OneGameAMonth.com.
